  • What is ELK? ELK is a stack of three open-source products: Elasticsearch, Logstash, and Kibana.
  • What are the benefits of using ELK? ELK provides powerful search, analysis, and visualization capabilities for log and event data.
  • Why should you use ELK? ELK is a popular choice for centralized logging and monitoring, SIEM events, enabling you to gain insights from your data and troubleshoot issues effectively.
  • ELK is a powerful tool for managing and analyzing large volumes of data in real-time and makes a great component in the data pipelines along with Kafka.
